A little bit about me

My programming journey began my senior year of high school when I decided to take a computer science class for fun. I have always loved the idea of being able to design and build my own projects (sparked by my LEGO obsession as a child), so I thought the class would be a great fit for me. I ended up loving it, so I decided to pursue a degree in Computer Science at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas after I graduated.

During my first couple years of university, I continued to develop my programming skills through the classes I was taking as well as working on my own small projects on the side. During my senior year I started interning at a fintech startup called Intellmind where I learned more about full-stack web development, cloud computing, and working in a team. I then went on to pass my AWS Cloud Practitioner certification to become more educated about the cloud computing fundamentals.

My goals are to continue learning more about full-stack development and cloud computing so that I can eventually land a full-time role as a software engineer. I have also become interested in machine learning, so I'm looking forward to working on some personal projects in that field as well.
